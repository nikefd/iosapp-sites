Data Collection

Our apps are designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

All app data (such as game progress, settings, and preferences) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Some of our apps offer in-app purchases. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

Our apps do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect any information from children. The apps are safe for users of all ages.
